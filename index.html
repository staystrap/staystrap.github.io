star Nice job deciding to go abroad!

Every student who has spent some time abroad knows the hassle of gathering information about the destination, about things to do, and where to stay. Many students, interns and other young people collected the information on these pages to make things easier for those who will follow them to their respective cities. They might even let you contact them directly!

Congratulations on deciding to step out of your home country or even your continent for some time!

You will be challenged and you will grow a lot personally.
But luckily you are not the first to start from scratch!
